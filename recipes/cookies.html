Cookies

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • butter
  • chocolate chips
  • nuts
  • dried fruit

How to bake cookies

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well with each addition.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually blend the dry ingredients into the creamed mixture.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips, nuts, and/or dried fruit.
  7. Drop rounded spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  8. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
